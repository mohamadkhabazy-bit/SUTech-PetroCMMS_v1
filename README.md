**PetroCMMS Pro** is a fully functional desktop CMMS (Computerised Maintenance Management System) designed for petrochemical‑like industrial environments. It includes asset management, work orders, preventive maintenance scheduling, spare parts inventory, labor management, and IE‑focused KPIs (MTBF, MTTR, OEE, downtime analysis).

---

## 🎓 Academic Context

This project was developed as part of the **Industrial Engineering curriculum** at **Shiraz University of Technology (SUTech)**. It demonstrates practical application of:

- Maintenance management principles (RCM, TPM)
- Industrial KPIs and performance measurement
- Database design and GUI development
- Software engineering for industrial systems

---

## ✨ Features

- **Asset Registry** – equipment data with criticality, status, MTBF/MTTR, health scoring.
- **Work Orders** – complete lifecycle (Open → In Progress → On Hold → Completed → Closed), priority & cost tracking.
- **PM Schedules** – preventive maintenance with frequency types and compliance alerts.
- **Spare Parts Inventory** – stock levels, min/max, reorder points, and valuation.
- **Labor Management** – workforce registry with trade, shift, hourly rate.
- **Dashboard & KPIs** – real‑time fleet health, open WOs, low stock, PM due, plus charts for status, criticality, downtime, cost breakdown.
- **Reports** – one‑click generation of operational reports (overdue PM, low stock, cost summary, etc.).
- **Professional UI** – navy blue theme consistent with SCADA/MES industrial systems.
- **Standalone executable** – single‑file `.exe` (Windows) – no Python installation needed.
